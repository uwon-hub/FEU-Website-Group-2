Situated in Sampaloc, Manila, is the Far Eastern University, abbreviated as FEU, which was established by Dr. Nicanor Reyes, Jr. in June 1928. It is, however, more than just a school; it is a place where dreams are molded. In its cost effective, state of the art equipment and well planned campus, we build an atmosphere that stimulates creativity and promotes collaboration. In addition to this, there are several co-curricular activities that the students can participate in that go a long way in expanding their horizons as well as their understanding. C. U. In accomplishing this goal FEU has an integrated approach to education offering rigorous academic instruction, work experience, great employment relations and quite an embracing engagement in the community with much diversity and inclusion so that everyone gets to flourish.
